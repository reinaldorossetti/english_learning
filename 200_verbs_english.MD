## Summary

This comprehensive guide covers 200 of the most commonly used verbs in English, organized into 10 semantic categories:

- **Fundamental Verbs (1-20)**: Core verbs essential for basic communication
- **Movement Verbs (21-40)**: Actions involving physical movement and location changes
- **Communication Verbs (41-60)**: Verbs for expressing ideas and exchanging information
- **Action Verbs (61-80)**: General action and manipulation verbs
- **Feeling Verbs (81-100)**: Emotional and psychological states
- **Work Verbs (101-120)**: Professional, educational, and economic activities
- **Daily Life Verbs (121-140)**: Routines and everyday activities
- **Experience Verbs (141-160)**: Perception, cognition, and agreement
- **Conclusion Verbs (161-180)**: Completion, conflict, and finalization
- **Advanced Verbs (181-200)**: Technical and sophisticated vocabulary

Each verb includes:
- ID number (1-200)
- Phonetic pronunciation
- Portuguese translation
- Conjugations (present, past, future)
- Example dialogue (question and answer)
- Brief explanation
- Associated emotion/context (emoji)

Use this guide to build a strong foundation in English vocabulary and improve your communication skills!
